About Diet Optimizer


Welcome to Diet Optimizer!


Diet Optimizer is committed to finding you the best recipes that suit your customized diet. We cater to all diets, restrictions, cuisines, etc.

By just entering some information about yourself, as well as the type of recipes you are looking for, Diet Optimizer will generate a list of recipes and foods from popular websites for you. Once logged in, visit the ‘Settings’ page to keep your preferences up-to-date and even save your go-to recipe settings.


Be sure to check out your ‘History’ page to revisit your favorite recipes and our ‘Most Famous Recipes’ page to see what recipes others are loving!
